Undergraduate Programmes

Open the door to sought-after technology careers with our world-class Bachelor of Science in Computer Science. The department offers two undergraduate degree programs: Bachelor of Science in Computer Science Single-Major (3:2:1:1) and Major-Minor (3:2:2:1), as well as Bachelor of Science in Information Technology.
To qualify for admission into Computer Science programs at Level 200, candidates must have completed specific courses at Level 100, including Algebra and Trigonometry, Calculus I, Introduction to Computer Science I, and Introduction to Computer Science II.

Graduate Programmes (MPhil & MSc)

Embrace world-class graduate education with a unique blend of cutting-edge research and industry experience. The Department of Computer Science at the University of Ghana offers Masters of Science (MSc.) and Master of Philosophy (MPhil.) in Computer Science.
The MSc. is a 1-year (weekend/evening) program with a capstone project, while the MPhil is a 2-year (full-time) program. The first year of the MPhil includes taught courses, and the second year focuses on research and dissertation. These accelerated master's programs are specially designed for both full-time and part-time study, equipping students with a broad knowledge of theoretical computer science and advanced technical skills.

Graduate Programmes (PhD)

Our Ph.D. programs lead the effort to extend the boundaries of knowledge through quality and industry-relevant research. This full-time research program has an expected duration of 4 years, requiring a high level of commitment
Students are expected to reside close to the University and actively participate in the program. Under the guidance of the postgraduate committee, Ph.D. students create a personalized program of study, including a major area of specialization and at least two minor areas of specialization.
In addition to coursework, the department hosts regular seminars, providing a platform for students and faculty to review and critique research. Students are encouraged to join a research lab, participating in activities related to Intelligent Space and Machine Learning, Data Science/Big Data Analytics, Networking and Communications Engineering, and Cyber Security.

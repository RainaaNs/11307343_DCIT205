Short Courses

Welcome to our Short Courses, designed to provide valuable skills and knowledge in key areas of technology. These courses are crafted to empower participants with practical insights and hands-on experience. Explore our offerings below :

1. Computer Network Fundamentals (CCNA)

Become a Cisco Certified Network Associate through our quality training program. In this course, participants will delve into the essentials of computer networking, gaining the skills needed to design, implement, and manage networks. Topics covered include:

-Networking Basics
-Routing and Switching
-Network Security
-Wireless Networking

Upon completion, participants will be well-prepared to take the CCNA certification exam.

2. Mobile Application Development

Unlock the world of Mobile App Development using the mobile-first worklight by IBM. This course equips participants with the tools and knowledge to create innovative and user-friendly mobile applications. Key topics covered include:

-Mobile App Design Principles
-Worklight Development Environment
-User Experience (UX) Design
-Mobile App Testing and Deployment

Get ready to explore the exciting field of mobile app development.

3. Introduction to Web Programming

Dive into the fundamentals of web programming and unleash your creativity in building web-based applications. This course provides a solid foundation in web development technologies, covering:

-HTML5 and CSS3
-JavaScript Programming
-Responsive Web Design
-Web Application Development

By the end of the course, participants will have the skills to create dynamic and interactive web applications.

4. Information Security and Ethical Hackiing

Explore the world of cybersecurity and ethical hacking in this comprehensive course. Participants will learn various techniques to secure computer systems and gain insights into ethical hacking practices. Key topics include:

-Cybersecurity Fundamentals
-Penetration Testing
-Cryptography
-Incident Response and Handling

Equip yourself with the knowledge to protect computer systems from potential threats.


How to Enroll

Enrolling in our short courses is easy. Visit our registration portal to sign up for the course of your choice. For more information, feel free to contact our course coordinator.

At the Computer Science Department, University of Ghana, we are committed to providing high-quality short courses that enhance your skills and prepare you for the dynamic field of computer science. Join us on a journey of continuous learning and innovation!
